How does Canada benefit from Asia?

Canada is a major beneficiary of Asia's economic growth. The increased demand for Canada's natural resources, such as oil and timber, has helped to drive up the value of the Canadian dollar, making exports to Asia more attractive. Canada has also benefited from increased trade in goods and services with Asia, particularly China, as well as from investment from Asian businesses. Canada has also been able to tap into Asia's growing consumer markets, with sales of Canadian-made products and services increasing in the region.
