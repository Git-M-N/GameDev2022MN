Economy Asia from 1900 to 2023

How did the Economy of Asia developed from 1900 to 2023? The economy of Asia has developed significantly since 1900, driven largely by advances in technology and industrialization. In the early 20th century, nations across Asia began to develop their own industrial sectors and infrastructure, which in turn helped to propel economic growth. After World War II, many Asian countries experienced rapid economic growth due to the implementation of economic reforms and the implementation of a market economy. During this period, Japan and the Four Asian Tigers (Hong Kong, Singapore, South Korea, and Taiwan) emerged as leading economies in Asia. In the decades since, countries across Asia have continued to grow their economies, often at a rapid rate. China and India, in particular, have experienced rapid economic expansion since the 1990s, leading to increased global trade and investment.
